01 — About

A native-first engineer who likes things polished.

I graduated in Computer Science in 2020 and started building for Apple platforms in early 2023. Since then I've focused on shipping production iOS and macOS apps with a strong bias toward native performance and clean architecture.

My early work centred on deep Google API integrations — Forms, Drive and Gmail — where I built full-featured iOS apps and extended them to macOS with Mac Catalyst. Those apps are still live and still evolving.

From there I moved into document tooling: PDF utilities and editors that handle formats like DOCX and XLS. Most recently I shipped MailPro, a full Gmail client for macOS, with Calendar and Contacts integrations on the way.

Away from the keyboard you'll find me playing indie games or the odd game of badminton — both of which keep me curious and competitive.
