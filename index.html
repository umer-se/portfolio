About Me .

I graduated in Software Engineering in 2020 and began my professional journey in Apple platform development in early 2023. Since then, I’ve focused on building and shipping production-ready iOS and macOS applications with a strong emphasis on native performance and clean architecture.

My early work centered around deep Google API integrations — including Google Forms, Drive, and Gmail — where I built full-featured iOS apps and later expanded them to macOS using Mac Catalyst. These applications remain live on the App Store and continue to evolve.

Over time, I expanded into document-focused applications, developing PDF tools and working with formats such as DOCX and XLS to build practical file management and editing solutions. Most recently, I’ve been leading the development of a full-featured Gmail client for macOS, with ongoing integrations for Calendar and Contacts APIs.

Outside of development, I enjoy playing indie games and occasionally badminton — interests that keep me curious, competitive, and always learning.
